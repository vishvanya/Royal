10 Best Restaurants to have Delicious Food in Mangalore

  • Post category:Restaurants / Travel

Mangalore is a city in the Karnataka state of India, is located on the coastal line and famous for beaches and temples, here the restaurants serve various sets of cuisines including Seafood, Chinese, American, and other popular and delicious dishes. The best Restaurants ranked below are rated by the travellers in online travel portals and by reviews of customers on services, taste, and interior of the Restaurants.

Sannadige


It is A very popular restaurant for locals and visitors alike for excellent traditional and seafood, people often like Appam, Seafood, and Ghee roast, Soup, and Tasty Veg Foods in Sannadige restaurant. This is the place to have a great experience with Mangalorean food, Nice ambience and Good view, the food you get worth for the money you pay.

Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, Vegetarian-Friendly
Meals: Lunch, Dinner, After-hours
Restaurant features: Reservations, Seating, Waitstaff, Parking Available, Valet Parking, Validated Parking, Wheelchair Accessible
Address: Goldfinch Hotel, Bunts Hostel Road, Near Jyoti Circle, Balmatta, Mangalore

Liquid Lounge


This is the excellent place for hangout on the weekend, is popular for foods and cocktails, the restaurant with pubs that offers a wide range of alcohol that mostly liked by youths as it offers cocktail, Music, and other relaxation options. The restaurant has smoking and non-smoking spaces. The non-smoking area is better for families and who like to smoke-free and clean air. The place is accessible easily from anywhere from the city.

Cuisine: Indian, Bar, Pub, Diner
Meals: Lunch, Dinner, After-hours, Drinks
Restaurant features: Seating, Television, Serves Alcohol
Address: Bharath Bldg, Balmatta Road, Next to Don Bosco Hall, Mangalore – 575001
Phone Number: 0824-425-5175

Gajalee Sea Food Restaurant

The Gajalee restaurant is located on the hilltop of Kadri Hills with imposing surroundings and spread across in a huge space. It is the best seafood restaurant and also serves other non-veg dishes with regional and other famous delicious cuisines. This restaurant is good for Business meetings, Child-friendly, Kids, View, Romantic, Groups. The Gajalee has a huge menu of cuisines including a variety of seafood, Chicken dishes, Mutton dishes, Squid and Clam, Rice preparations, Indian Bread, Soups, Thali, Chinese Soups and other variety of foods.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner, Brunch, After-hours
Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, Gluten-Free Options
Restaurant features: Delivery, Takeout, Reservations, Outdoor Seating, Seating, Waitstaff, Parking Available, Validated Parking, Highchairs Available, Serves Alcohol, Full Bar.
Hours: Open all days, 11 am to 3 pm & 7 pm till midnight
Address: Gajalee, Circuit House Compound, Kadri Hills, Mangalore 575004
Phone: 0824-222-1900

Pallkhi Restaurant

It is an award-winning restaurant by NDTV and one of the restaurants in India. The restaurant offers good Vegetarian and Non-vegetarians cuisines, the chicken dishes here popular among the other dishes, it offers excellent services with skilled staffs.

Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, Vegetarian-Friendly, Vegan Options
Meals: Lunch, Dinner, After-hours
Restaurant features: Delivery, Takeout, Reservations, Seating, Waitstaff, Parking Available, Serves Alcohol, Full Bar
Popular Foods: Soups, Bar, Starters, Vegetarian, Chicken dishes, Fish, Cocktail, Mocktail, and other variety of veg and non-veg foods.
Address: balmatta road, | opp Jyothi cinema, next to KMC, Mangalore
Phone: 0824-244-4929

Sizzler’s Ranch

Sizzler Ranch is the best and comfortable restaurant offers dishes with Veg and Non-veg. The restaurant has breakfast, reservation and takeout, ideal for child-friendly, Romantic, Local Cuisine and Outdoor Seating.

Cuisine: Vegetarian-Friendly
Meals: Dinner, Lunch, After-hours
The restaurant features: Outdoor Seating, Seating, Waitstaff, Takeout, Reservations
Address: Nehru Avenue Road, Mangalore – 575003
Phone: +91 8242459008

The Ocean Pearl

Ocean Pearl is the Hotel and Restaurants and Sagar Ratna is named for inside the restaurant of ocean pearl, has fine maintained Veg and Non-veg cuisine restaurants. It is good for Seafood, Delicacies, North Indian, Local delights and mughalai, has an authentic cuisine, variety of caters prepared here.

Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, Vegetarian-Friendly, Vegan Options, Gluten-Free Options
Meals: Dinner, Breakfast, Lunch, After-hours
Restaurant features: Reservations, Seating, Parking Available, Validated Parking, Valet Parking, Wheelchair Accessible, Serves Alcohol, Takeout, Waitstaff, Full Bar
Address: Navabharath circle, Mangalore – 575003
Phone: +91 8242413800

Madhuvan’s Village

Surrounded by a green garden, the restaurant feels inside the real village as they designed interior like village home. This restaurant is well known for seafood including local and continental. The restaurant has ample and valet parking facility. The Restaurant offers a Cocktail and Mocktail. In cocktails: a concoction of Vodka, mint, lime, and sugar and Bloody Mary, a combination of Vodka, Tomato Juice, Tabasco, Lime, and Worcester. In Mocktails: Vanilla, Strawberry, milk, Granadine Syrup and fresh fruits and Sky blue – with Litchi, milk, blue Curacao, litchi crush, Pistachio ice cream.

Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, International, Chinese, Vegetarian-Friendly
Meals: Lunch, Dinner, After-hours
Restaurant features: Highchairs Available, Wheelchair Accessible, Takeout, Reservations, Outdoor Seating, Seating, Waitstaff, Parking Available, Validated Parking, Television, Serves Alcohol, Accepts Mastercard, Accepts Visa, Full Bar
Address: kadri, bondel road, Mangalore – 575008
Phone: +91 8242214158

Mangala Restaurant

Best for seafood and other non-veg cuisines, it offers a cocktail with other variety of alcohol. Mangala restaurant is the place to have Mangalorean local foods, you can expect other mouth-watering foods and other entertainment options inside the restaurants.

Cuisine: North Indian, Chinese, Mangalorean
Meals: Lunch, Dinner
Restaurant features: Delivery, Takeout, Seating, Waitstaff, Home Delivery, Full Bar Available, Live Music, Smoking Area
Address: Sliva Cross Road, Fathima Retreat House, Mangalore 575002
Phone: 0824-243-7026, 0824-243-6726

Maharaja Restaurant

Maharaja Restaurant offers seafood and a variety of local and style Non-veg dishes. It is the best gateway for the family at the weekend to have delicious seafood, one must try for sure.

Cuisine: Indian, Seafood, Asian, Vegetarian-Friendly
Meals: Lunch, Dinner, After-hours
Restaurant features: Takeout, Reservations, Seating, Waitstaff, Delivery, Parking Available, Full Bar
Address: Trade Centre, 1st Floor, Jyothi Circle, Bunts Hostel Road, Balmatta, Mangalore – 575001
Phone: 0824-244-1526

Barbeque Nation


This is the Veg and Non-Veg restaurant offers cuisines such as Indian, Barbecue, Mediterranean, Asian, Vegan Options and Vegetarian-Friendly. The best place to hang out with family and friends for lunch and dinner and the price is slightly highest-end when compared to another restaurant. Reservation is needed a day before as Barbeque Nation busy as it reserved every table most of the day. Good place for Groups, Child-friendly, Kids.

Cuisine: Indian, Barbecue, Mediterranean, Asian, Vegan Options, Vegetarian-Friendly
Meals: Lunch, Dinner
Restaurant features: Reservations, Seating, Waitstaff, Wheelchair Accessible
Address: 3rd Floor, Mak Mall, Kankanady, Mangalore 570002
Phone: +91 8246060008
